Golden-Era

Fitness Application with a body-building orientated theme of the "Golden Era" Features: - Stat tracking (Track your lifts) - Personal Trainer (Tailored workout and meal plans) - Exercise and Workout programme library - Export data for use with Mobile Applications (Coming Soon...)
